# Sample AI Revenue Leak Scan

Target: Example landing page for a $49 SaaS onboarding checklist.

Audience: Seed-stage SaaS founders who need to improve trial activation.

Conversion goal: More visitors should start checkout for the checklist.

## Leak 1: The headline sells a document, not an outcome

Why it costs revenue: "Get our onboarding checklist" asks buyers to value the artifact. A founder under pressure is more likely to buy a result than a file.

Fix: Replace the headline with "Find the 7 onboarding gaps that quietly kill paid activation."

## Leak 2: Proof appears below the first checkout button

Why it costs revenue: The page asks for payment before giving the buyer a reason to trust the checklist.

Fix: Move one concrete proof point above the first CTA: "Built from 42 SaaS onboarding audits; includes the exact checks we use before recommending product work."

## Leak 3: The CTA does not reduce uncertainty

Why it costs revenue: "Buy now" creates a small commitment cliff when the buyer still wonders what is inside.

Fix: Change the CTA to "Get the checklist and 30-minute activation pass" and add a one-line note beside it: "Instant PDF, 14 pages, refund if it does not surface one fix."

## Quick Win

Ship the new headline and proof point before sending more traffic. Those two changes improve clarity and trust without touching the product or checkout.
